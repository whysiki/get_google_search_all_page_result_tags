Big Jobs, Small Jobs, Every Job.

From arranging fully-functional temporary spaces to installing a complete HVAC, we’ve got you covered. We understand that big jobs are stressful. We’ll be in touch every step of the way, keeping you updated on all you need to know to keep your job headache free. And there’s no job too big: we’ll fly in a helicopter if we need to. Facility maintenance happens. Windows break, locks need changing, walls need painting, hinges need tightening — and that’s just the tip of the iceberg. Everybody goes through it. It’s how you deal with it that makes a difference. Call us and we’ll have someone on the scene to take care of it before you can put up an “excuse the mess” sign. Our job is to keep your stores up and running. When you’ve got a problem, we’ll take care of it. From the first service call until we personally confirm with you that the job’s been completed, we make sure the process is smooth. We handle all the invoices and the phone calls and the insurance forms for you and keep the pile of paperwork on your desk to a minimum.

Putting Clients First

From the beginning, the foundation of our customer-oriented service has been based on a simple value, one that’s reflected even in the company’s name, BOSS: Built On Superior Service. We base every decision and action taken by every representative of the company on this core philosophy, and it’s the reason business has grown every year since our founding in 2001: our service is is superior. Businesses are built on relationships. We believe our dedication to providing fair prices and clear communication — every step of the way — is the reason we’re able to form the quality relationships we have with our clients. And that’s been proven by the fact that we’ve never lost a client to a competitor.

We Go Where Our Clients Go

It’s important that our company have as wide a geographic reach as our clients do. That’s why we’ve formed relationships with over 4,000 strategic alliance partners from all over the country, chosen because they share our sense of corporate integrity and service standards. We make sure each lives up to the rigorous standards we do, and that they abide by all applicable safety and compliance guidelines. Our partners span the continental U.S., as well as Canada, Hawaii, and Puerto Rico, so we go wherever our clients go. With the ability to provide service on this large a scale, we know our clients will never be left in the dark.
