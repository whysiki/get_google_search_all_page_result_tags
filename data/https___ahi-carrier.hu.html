Willis Carrier invented modern air conditioning in 1902

Before the invention of the modern air conditioner, the heat and humidity often resulted in lost working hours and awake nights. On July 17, 1902, Willis Haviland Carrier designed the first modern air conditioning system, thus starting an industry that fundamentally improved the way we live, work and have fun.
