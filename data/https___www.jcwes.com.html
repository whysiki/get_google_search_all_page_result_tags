Electrical Testing & Inspections

JCW Energy Services provide electrical testing services and compliance inspections across the UK mainland. Industries we work with include schools, universities and government bodies in the public sector, as well as the leisure, retail, commercial and industrial sectors.

Statutory Compliance

Regular maintenance plays a vital part in maintaining health and safety standards that are conducive to creating working environments that are safe and comfortable for employees and occupants. That's why we offer our clients statutory compliance services.

 
History

location and coverage

JCW operate from 8 offices across the UK, allowing us to provide regionalised services. All of our sites benefit from knowledgeable staff trained to the highest industry standards that are able to demonstrate the benefits of experience, continuity and technical expertise. With 180 directly employed engineers on board we have the UK covered.

 

what makes us unique

JCW Energy Services provide a wide range of self-delivered services which are complemented by professional managers providing bespoke solutions to our customers.

We have a large number of directly employed engineers with an even split between electrically and mechanically qualified engineers, many of whom have worked with us through their apprenticeships to becoming specialists in their fields.

We ensure that our people are responsive and accessible providing an exceptional customer service.

Featured

JCW Projects provides a professional HVAC design and installation service, including full turnkey project management to bespoke design and installation.  No project is too large or small and can include a single unit installation or R22 replacement through to a major office refurbishment or the mechanical installation to a new build project.

Featured

JCW Maintenance not only supports the completed installation projects but also provides a full mechanical and electrical planned and reactive maintenance service to single buildings or national multi-site customers.  Each contract is tailored to meet our client’s specific needs and can include maintenance for one service such as electrical testing to a complete hard facilities management maintenance regime.
