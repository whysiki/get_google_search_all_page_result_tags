About Cullum

Established in 1972, Cullum now consists of two flourishing subsidiaries: Cullum Constructors, Inc. and Cullum Services, Inc. Over its extensive history, Cullum’s notable growth has enabled the organization to secure unlimited licenses in mechanical  and general construction in South Carolina, along with mechanical and plumbing licenses in North Carolina and Georgia. Cullum specializes in commercial plumbing and HVAC service, repair, maintenance and installation for commercial systems. Cullum is capable of effectively managing projects of all sizes and scopes with flexibility and comprehensive support. Contact Cullum today to discuss your upcoming projects.
