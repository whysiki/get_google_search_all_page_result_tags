      Innovative heat exchange solutions

      Digitalisation, green transition of industries and growing populations with rising standards of living demand sustainable solutions. In nearly all industries, heat exchangers play a pivotal role for efficient process operation. Heat rejection and heat recovery are essential to increase the overall efficiency of cooling and heating systems.

      HVAC

      Our high-quality heat exchangers for the HVAC industry are specially designed to ensure maximum performance and efficiency. With years of experience in the industry, we offer a wide range of heat exchangers for various applications in the heating, ventilation and air conditioning industry. Our products are characterized by their durable construction, reliability and energy efficiency.

      Data Centre

      In the digital era, data centres are the backbone of the information society. Constant and efficient cooling of data centres is essential to ensure the reliability and performance of sensitive hardware. Data centre air conditioning is not only a question of functionality, but also of energy efficiency and sustainability. We at Kelvion attach great importance to this. Our technological edge and the maximum reliability and durability of our heat exchangers ensure worry-free use for many years.

      Read more about Data Centre Solutions
      Food & Beverage

      As a key technology in the food and beverage industry, heat exchangers are essential for ensuring product quality and safety as they enable precise temperature control, for example. In addition, these systems enable manufacturers to create optimal conditions for processing and storage while increasing energy efficiency. The innovative technology and materials of our heat exchangers not only guarantee efficient production, but also compliance with optimum hygiene standards. They have been specially designed for use in hygienically sensitive areas and therefore support your sustainable production.

      Read more about Food & Beverage Solutions
      Hydrogen

      In the world of renewable energy, green hydrogen plays a key role, especially in Germany, where the production and manufacture of green hydrogen is being driven forward using advanced technologies. By using electrolysis processes that utilize renewable energy sources, water is split into hydrogen and oxygen. Leading technologies in this field include alkaline electrolysis (AEC) and proton exchange membrane electrolysis (PEMEC), both of which are known for their efficiency in the environmentally friendly production of hydrogen. Kelvion also contributes to sustainable energy production with its innovative heat exchange solutions.

      Read more about Hydrogen Solutions
      Power / Renewables

      More than half of the increase in global energy demand is used to generate electricity. Natural gas and renewable energy sources will increasingly replace coal. Renewable energies account for more than a third of the growth in electricity generation. A similar trend can be seen in the growing global demand for primary energy, a quarter of which could be covered by renewable energies. Kelvion has the right solutions for the generation and supply of energy. Our products are used in various power plants around the world. Regardless of global trends, there will always be a demand for our expertise and knowledge of the fuels that power the world economy.

      Read more about Power Solutions
      Transportation

      In a world in which mobility is playing an increasingly central role, the need for efficient heat exchangers for the transportation industry is also growing steadily. Especially in regions with strong economic growth such as Asia, the demand for innovative solutions for temperature control and optimization on roads, rails and in the air is higher than ever. With this in mind, our mission at Kelvion is to meet the challenges of the transportation industry while shaping a sustainable future.

      Read more about Transportation Solutions
      Chemicals

      The growing purchasing power of consumers, particularly in Asia, is increasing the demand for chemicals and the products made from them. At the same time, dwindling oil and gas reserves are driving the search for new raw materials such as biomass. The biggest challenge facing the industry is to help the world's growing population to maintain and improve their standard of living in the long term. In the ever-evolving world of chemistry, Kelvion plays a crucial role by providing efficient heat exchanger solutions for the industry's diverse challenges.

      Read more about Chemical Solutions
      LNG

      Our company specializes in the sale of heat exchangers for the LNG shipping industry. Our high-quality heat exchangers enable efficient and reliable operation of LNG ships by optimizing the cooling process and increasing energy efficiency.

      Read more about LNG Solutions
      Supermarkets

      Refrigeration technology is at the heart of every supermarket - it enables retailers to stock a wide range of perishable goods all year round, ensuring the freshness and quality of the wide assortment. From fruit and vegetables, meat and dairy products to pre-packed meals and frozen foods, it is important that temperatures are kept constant in the cold chain to ensure food safety and avoid food waste. Kelvion stands for refrigeration technology solutions that are tailored to the specific requirements of the food retail industry and at the same time focus on energy efficiency and sustainability.
