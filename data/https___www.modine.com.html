We Are Modine

For more than 100 years, Modine Manufacturing Company has been leading the way in thermal management. We provide the commercial, industrial and vehicular markets with trusted systems and solutions to manage heating and cooling and improve air quality. Were at work in practically every corner of the world, inside the things you see every day.
