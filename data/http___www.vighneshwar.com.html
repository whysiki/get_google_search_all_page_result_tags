Welcome To

Vighneshwar Airconditioning Pvt. Ltd.

Vighneshwar Air Conditioning Private Limited is a progressive HVAC project engineering company. We provide cutting-edge technological solutions to clients belonging to Pharmaceutical, Banking, Textile, Automobile, Hospitality, Information Technology and other sectors.

The entity was founded in 1995 with a goal to provide qualitative HVAC solutions. With over two decades of experience, we have established ourselves at the forefront in the HVAC domain, as an engineering solutions provider.

We undertake all kinds of Turnkey Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning projects such as Precision Air Conditioning, Centralized Air Conditioning Systems, Clean Rooms, Mortuary, Industrial Ventilation systems, Variable Refrigerant Flow, etc., and provide appropriate sales and service solutions.
