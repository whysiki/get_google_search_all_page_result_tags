We follow a rigorous 4 'I' approach to everything HVAC

Interact

This is the stage where we listen to you. The team of designers at our company pays utmost attention to understand your heating, ventilation, and centralized air conditioning requirements. We take building drawings and layouts to comprehend the building dimensions and features that enable us to innovate and design a customized HVAC solution catered to your specific needs.

 

Innovate & Design

Whether it’s a greenfield turnkey HVAC project or a brownfield ongoing HVAC project, innovation and design play an important role not only in delivering an efficient and aesthetic HVAC solution but also in reducing the CAPEX as well as OPEX. The team of designers at our company is adept at handling large size, medium size, small size projects with equal ease. We spend a considerable amount of time in innovation and design in order to deliver an energy efficient HVAC solution at the lowest price point to our customers.

 

Install

Once the design is approved and the work order is issued, our team of installers quickly get into the groove. We select installers based on geography, ratings, and the team size. The installation team works under the supervision of our design engineer and installs the HVAC equipment and machinery as per the approved design and drawing.

 

Impress

After the installation work is completed within the given timeframe, our team of design engineers thoroughly test the HVAC system. Only when the HVAC system has passed through a series of stringent quality checks, we hand it over to the client.

The best kilowatt of 'renewable' energy generated is the kilowatt that you save through 'energy efficiency'

Do you know that an HVAC system consumes nearly 60% of energy for any given building? Whether it’s a warehouse, cold storage, hospital, shopping mall, office building, restaurant, food court, recreational center, fitness center, showroom, boutique, clinic, startup office space, shop, the bulk of the electricity is consumed in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning. This is where our design-centric approach helps our clients in achieving energy efficiency resulting in reduced electricity bills.

For large buildings such as malls, hospitals, office buildings, warehouses, the yearly savings on electricity bills run into Crore of Rupees. At the same time, with the right HVAC design, we also bring down the upfront capital expenditure for our clients. Right HVAC design includes precise load calculations, understanding the usage pattern of the HVAC system, use of controls, use of sensors, and automation wherever applicable. With this comprehensive design approach, we are able to reduce CAPEX / OPEX for our clients.
