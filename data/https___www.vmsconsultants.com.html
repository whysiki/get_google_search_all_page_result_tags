WHO WE
ARE

VMS is a leading consultancy firm offering comprehensive engineering, architecture, and project management services. With a proud legacy of over 60 years and a portfolio spanning 2,000+ projects, VMS was founded in 1963 by Dr. Rasvihari Vakil, Dr. Mahendra Mehta, and Mr. Ramesh Sheth as a pioneering, research-driven organization.

Headquartered in Ahmedabad, our reach extends across India and international market segments. With a dedicated team of over 100+ professionals, we bring together expertise from diverse disciplines to serve leading Indian and international corporations, as well as public sector organizations.
