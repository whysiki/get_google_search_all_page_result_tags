ABOUT PROMATEK

Promatek is a leading HVAC company in Nigeria that executes medium to large scale engineering and construction projects from feasibility through design and contract administration, to project handover, in high profile engineering-based industries.

We expose hidden opportunities in the Carbonated Soft Drinks (CSD), Water, Juice, Energy and Dairy Categories, using experience trends, and projections for the future.  We give a picture of the competitive landscape and likely threats as well as detailed theoretical and empirical view of Route To Market (RTM) and Distribution Systems.
Our work provides detailed report that will bridge the knowledge and decision-ready-information gap.
As a leading HVAC company in Nigeria, Promatek is aware that every year, organizations roll out strategic initiatives in response to competition, market demand, environment and other economic forces. Such initiatives are often realized through the institution of projects. The time to realizing profitable revenue or other project objectives depends on how effectively the projects are executed. Successful projects are rewarding but still remain a challenge for many organizations.
Promatek understands the critical role of knowledge based decisions, effective project execution, and Human Capital Development in an organization’s life cycle. Promatek is therefore a response to the need for a crop of knowledgeable and skillful human resource base, capable of delivering best-in-class results for organizations’ projects implementation, especially in the Food & Beverage Industry.
