Wits In 60 Seconds

Halala Witsies! Wits celebrated its top academics and dedicated staff at the 2024 Vice-Chancellor's Awards. The Postgraduate Symposium showcased the impactful research of Master’s and PhD students from all faculties. We catch up with GENUS Africa and Ancient Odysseys who offer immersive palaeontology vacations in SA, allowing participants to contribute to fossil digs. We meet the Wits EcoCane Innovations team that won the 2024 NIC with their sugarcane-derived biodegradable bioplastic. Finally, we explore the free WorkReady Gen Z online course to help students develop essential skills for a smooth transition from university to the world of work.

Sarhana Dinat | (2nd, PhD Gradflash) is a PhD candidate in Pharmaceutical Microbiology at the Department of Pharmacy and Pharmacology, focusing on the gut pathogen Helicobacter pylori.
