Northern New Jersey HVAC Company

Every home or business in our vibrant region tells its own story, and each one has unique heating, cooling, and air quality needs. At Reiner Group, Inc., we listen to these stories and respond with tailored solutions to keep homes comfortable year-round.

Our team of seasoned professionals brings a deep understanding of the local climate and top-notch technical skills to every job. Our goal goes beyond simply providing a service—we make sure our neighbors can enjoy their homes and workplaces, whatever the weather. 

Reiner can offer our customers the benefits of professional engineers, plumbers, electricians, mechanics, and technicians who are licensed to perform work in multiple states. Therefore, we can provide complete design-build services to meet your individual needs.

Choosing Reiner means choosing a company that genuinely cares about your family or business. You are part of our community, and your satisfaction matters to us. We’re committed to delivering personable, professional service without jargon, hidden fees, or rushed service. 

Make Reiner your Northern New Jersey HVAC company today.

Quality Products from Reputable Brands 

We believe in providing our clients with only the best, so we offer products from industry leaders like Lennox, Rheem, Honeywell, and Mitsubishi Electric. These brands are renowned for their innovation, reliability, and energy efficiency. Whether you’re looking for a powerful air conditioner, a dependable furnace, a smart thermostat, or an advanced air purifier, these brands have you covered. Their top-tier products enhance your home’s comfort and contribute to energy savings and a healthier living environment.

Every home is unique, with its own heating and cooling needs. That’s why, in addition to offering products from leading brands, we’re also equipped to service all makes and models of HVAC systems. Whether your system is old or new, our experienced professionals have the skills and knowledge to ensure it’s running at its best.

Our commitment to excellence, ability to service all HVAC systems, and partnerships with reputable brands make us the best choice for your HVAC needs in Northern New Jersey.

HVAC emergencies don’t stick to regular business hours, and neither do we.

Whether it’s a sudden air conditioning system breakdown on a sweltering summer day or an unexpected furnace failure during the coldest winter night, we’re here for you.

Where We've Been, Where We Are, and Where We're Going

Past

Reiner and Company was originally established by William Reiner in 1934 in Fair Lawn, NJ. Bernard Tolsma Sr. acquired the company in 1969 and continued to grow the business instilling core values such as hard work and respect.

Present

Reiner Group, Inc. continues to operate out of Fair Lawn, NJ in the same location it was founded. Bernard Tolsma Jr. followed in his father’s footsteps and is our company president. Reiner Group currently employs over 150 talented people.

Future

We are excited about what the future will bring for Reiner and to our industry. We will continue to train and stay up-to-date with the latest HVAC technologies to better serve our customers.
