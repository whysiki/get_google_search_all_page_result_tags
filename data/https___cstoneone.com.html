commercial plumbing

From hotels, restaurants and apartment buildings to hospitals and schools, CornerStone One is a key partner in all types of commercial projects in Southeastern Wisconsin, including governmental. Big project or small, our professionals are committed to quality, on-time delivery.

prefab

Prefabricated plumbing systems offer greater precision and speed to completion, which make them the preferred method for many customers. All CornerStone One prefabricated systems are engineered and built in our 25,000-square-foot Brookfield plant, then shipped, assembled and installed on-site. Our accurate and efficient approach reduces costs and installation time, resulting in faster certificate of occupancy.

commercial hvac

Because we design, build, install and maintain the entire commercial HVAC system, we’re also able to ensure that everything is done right. From the first sketch to the last rivet, CornerStone One customers count on our HVAC division to deliver optimal and reliable function in their commercial systems.

earthwork

The Earthwork division of CornerStone One is a turnkey solution that provides unmatched quality, service and innovation in excavation, grading and site preparation. We’re a single, trusted resource that takes the time to know a site, a project and the specific needs of every client to ensure absolute excellence from start to finish.

site utilities

More than 30 years of professional experience allows our in-house site utility division to do what others can’t. With the highest quality of service and standards, CornerStone One is ready for anything: from sanitary, water, fire to sewer, our certified staff overcomes any challenge a project might unearth.

services/special projects

It seems that no two projects are exactly alike. Whether large or small, routine or truly unique, we have the talent, equipment and know-how to get the job done right the first time. We apply careful upfront analysis and planning to ensure functionality, timely delivery and maximum cost-effectiveness – while working out ahead of potential issues to prevent problems down the line.
