Always Up to Date Certificates

  • Ditch the paper pads! Powered Now has hundreds of pre built forms and certificates including everything for Plumbing and heating engineers (such as CP12’s, GASSAFE), Electricians (Zs Calculator, EIC's, MEIC's, PAT testing, and more) for building contractors and Oil and Renewable firms.
  • Filing cabinets are a thing of the past, everything is stored online and backed up.
  • Your customers are kept up to date and can access their certificates through the secure Powered Now portal.
  • Forms & Certificates including heating, plumbing, electrical, pest control and many more.
  • All Forms and Certificates are updated as soon as standards change meaning you don't need to worry about staying compliant.

Quoting & Invoicing

  • Create, send, and track professional looking quotes that customers can easily approve online. You can swiftly transform approved quotes into jobs and invoices ready for immediate dispatch or scheduling at a later date.
  • Generate precise and professional invoices instantly. Create a customer-friendly template with your company branding, contact details, and a personalised message and T&Cs.
  • Larger jobs can be easily managed within Powered Now’s Projects including easy to use workflow keeping everyone updated.
  • Customers can approve quotes, request modifications, or pay invoices through the Powered Now secure portal.

Job Management & Scheduling

  • Drag and Drop Diary - Effortlessly reschedule plumbing jobs or reassign tasks to another plumber by simply clicking, dragging, and dropping. A notification with all the necessary details will be sent to their phone.
  • Create the quickest and most fuel-efficient routes to maximise your daily workload. In the event of an emergency plumbing job, promptly assign it to the nearest engineer using their GPS.
  • View team member schedules simultaneously to identify availability, select an open time slot, and promptly schedule the work with a single tap.
  • Keep Customers Updated - Customers can track appointments through the Powered Now Portal, where they can review details, see assigned team members, and confirm or request changes if necessary.

Keep On Top of Finances

  • Get paid quickly both online and in person. Powered Now integrates with SumUp card readers and online payment gateways giving your customers the ability to spread payments while you get paid quickly.
  • Keep on top of your Supplier Invoices, Costs and Expenses. Costs can also be assigned to customers and projects at the click of a button.
  • No need for an expensive Accountant - You can submit your Tax Returns directly to HMRC with our easy to use Making Tax Digital feature.
  • Full integration into Xero Accounting, or export your data to use in a product of your choice.
