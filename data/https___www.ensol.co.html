Shalby Indore

Successful execution of first major Chilled Water project for EnSol at Shalby Hospitals, Indore propelled the company into limelight. EnSol held under its scope, complete supply & installation works of 600 TR Capacity Water Cooled Chillers along related auxiliaries like Pumping System, Cooling Towers, Tanks, Air-Handling Units, Fan Coil Units and Ventilation Systems. Despite being a young company, the project was handed-over before the stipulated deadline.

Rajpath Club

The most prestigious recreational clubs in Ahmedabad with a well-respected name in the country, has EnSol as its go-to organization for its HVAC requirements. EnSol started by successfully handing over Basement Ventilation System for 1,00,000 sq.ft. Parking Space in record time. Due to satisfactory services, currently, the company holds the Annual Maintenance Contract of all the Air-Conditioners of Rajpath Club Ltd. (1000 TR) while carrying out regular HVAC projects in the club.

Mega Metro

EnSol proudly holds the responsibility to provide HVAC services at multiple Ahmedabad Metro Stations with most efficient Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) Systems (more than 1000 HP Capacity). Out of a vast variety of healthy competition in the region, EnSol was selected as the suitable contractor considering its track record, meticulous execution and young aggressive team.

Sanraj Polyprinters

Wet-Ventilation of 35,000 Sq. Ft. of functional Printing Press Area is a challenge in itself and making it completely autonomous takes the project to a whole different level. EnSol Designed and Executed a completely automated 1,00,000 CFM Air-Washer System (Direct Evaporative Cooling) with Exhaust Systems, capable of altering all the involved parameters automatically as well as manually through a user-friendly Human-Machine Interface in the Programmable Logic Controller based Electrical Panel. The project has been leap for the company in the niche segment of HVAC Automation.
