It Feels Right – And It Is

We believe that service is key, so we ensure that the air conditioning service we offer is always excellent.

From project notification, through site survey, specification, quotation, and installation to completion of a project and beyond, our customers have come to expect exceptional levels of reliability from Aura.

A leading player in the UK HVAC marketplace, we operate nationwide from our locations in Derby and Coventry; view our case studies page to see completed projects.

Working closely with fit-out contractors at all project stages, we bring a level of expertise that provides value-engineered solutions delivering optimum performance in commercial air conditioning.

Why Choose Aura Air Conditioning?

 

Specialising in refrigerant systems, we use advanced design software and a team of highly skilled installers and have earned the confidence of contractors UK-wide over more than 20 years. Throughout every project, our operations personnel liaise closely with your project managers to ensure the smooth fulfilment of requirements.

Creating the right environment in which to operate is an important consideration for every organisation. Achieving a comfortable and stimulating interior climate is an essential part of this – always maintaining a suitable temperature with good air circulation.

Our exceptional level of expertise enhances climates with minimum disruption and fast, reliable project timescales. When you place an enquiry with us you get first-rate care and technical support from the outset.

This extends on to our professional air conditioning maintenance packages and nationwide preventative servicing and repair to keep your climatic requirements at peak performance.
