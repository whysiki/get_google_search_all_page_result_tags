      Making Homes More Comfortable Since 1885

      T.J. Fannon & Sons is dedicated to providing the best HVAC services in Northern Virginia. Find out the difference working with a company with over a century of experience makes!

      Our Quality Services

      Thomas J. Fannon & Sons can make sure you stay warm and comfortable. We service and install high-performance furnaces, boilers, and heat pumps with excellent efficiency ratings. We also offer service plans with comprehensive coverage. Our services can help you get the most out of your heating system.

      We service and install air conditioning systems and heat pumps that will keep you cool and comfortable, no matter how hot it gets outside. Thomas J. Fannon & Sons offers our customers the latest in cooling technology, with the highest SEER (Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio) ratings on the market.

      Whether you heat your domestic water with gas, oil, or electricity, we stock heaters for rapid replacement.

      We take pride in our responsive reliable deliveries by our experienced staff. Personal service is provided to every customer.

      Helping Northern Virginia Homeowners Since 1885

      Thomas J. Fannon & Sons is a family-owned HVAC company that has been in business for over 135 years. We are an established company that’s known for our professionalism and attention to detail. We provide the widest range of home comfort products and services in Northern Virginia. We install and service air conditioning systems, heat pumps and gas equipment. We also deliver home heating oil. Our expertise and experience is unmatched in our local area.

      Our Services

      • Heating – We specialize in servicing, repairing and installing furnaces and boilers. Our services can improve the performance of your current heating system and increase its lifespan by reducing the risk of system breakdowns. We can also replace your unit with a high-performance system to improve your home’s energy efficiency.
      • Cooling – Our air conditionersand ductless split systems ensure that your home will stay comfortable on the hottest day. Our experienced technicians can repair any kind of cooling problem as well.
      • Water Heaters – Few things are more unpleasant than finding water all over the basement floor. Call us (with a make and serial number) to determine its age, and replace that old unit before it leaks. We also offer tankless water heatersand indirect water heaters.
      • Fuel Oil – We are a full service fuel company, meaning that as a customer you are entitled to substantial discounts on repair service and parts.  No one can care for your equipment more reliably or at a better price than your full service oil dealer.

      At Thomas J. Fannon & Sons, we always have your best interests at heart and we will never recommend any products and services that you don’t need. We will honor our promises and do exactly what you hired us to do. We’ll be there when you need us, and we have over 100 years of experience and expertise backing that promise.
