When to Invest in a New Unit

An efficient HVAC system is something many of us take for granted. When your system is working properly, you don’t even think about it. However, if it fails, you notice right away. The following are a few common signs that you could need a new heating or cooling unit:

  • Your HVAC system isn’t properly heating or cooling your home.
  • Your system is using more power to do its job, costing you more in energy bills.
  • Unusual noises or smells are coming from your HVAC unit.

If you’re noticing any of these problems, it’s time to call in a professional to get your unit repaired or replaced as soon as possible.
