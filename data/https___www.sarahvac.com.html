WELCOME TO OUR SITE
Sara HVAC is a Mumbai based HVAC system provider company.

Sara HVAC Systems is headed by Sanjay Kumar Verma, having engineering background and an MBA from Madras University, having more than 17years rich experience in the field of HVAC Industry at various locations. He was also involved in execution of various Green Building and prestigious projects during his career as employee. It includes implementing Turnkey HVAC Projects for numerous industries like BPOs, Hospitals, Software, Hospitality industry etc. to various residences of high net worth individuals & professionals.

Today Sara HVAC caters to all sectors of industries such as BPO’s call centre industries, hotels and restaurants, super markets, clubs, guest Houses, banks, electronic media and FM, auditorium, offices, showrooms and multiplex etc. with the resources and facilities at our disposal, We can provide a wide spectrum of services in this highly specialized field and related fields.

We also have our business & service associates in Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka & Tamil Nadu.We have a dedicated team undertaking regular maintenance and servicing jobs for all its installations. Our engineers and technicians are intensively trained to handle the costly, sophisticated air-conditioning installations at client sites. We aim to provide total comfort and peace of mind that incorporates safety, efficiency and economy.
