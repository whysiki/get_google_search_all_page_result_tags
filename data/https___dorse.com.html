About Dorse

We arent just a parts supplier. Dorse is an HVAC manufacturer representative and a wholesale distribution company servicing mechanical engineers, contractors, and building owners. With a complete understanding of the construction life cycle, we’ve built specialized teams to deliver exactly what our customers need at exactly the right time. From HVAC project estimating at any stage through ordering to parts tracking and delivery, our pride in a job well done is priority number one.

Our Approach

As an HVAC distributor for the Pacific Northwest, we approach each project by learning as much as we can about our customers needs. Then, we apply decades of HVAC supplier experience, lean on our specialized teams for expertise and create the right solution. Service doesnt stop at the sale. Shipment tracking, follow-up and jobsite delivery are standard procedures in a job well done by Dorse.
